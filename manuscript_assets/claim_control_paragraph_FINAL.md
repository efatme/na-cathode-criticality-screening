# Reviewer-facing claim-control paragraph

This study presents a reproducible, post-DFT decision-support workflow for prioritizing sodium-ion cathode candidates using computed electrode records, conservative criticality-aware triage, text-mined literature evidence, and leakage-aware grouped cross-validation. It does not experimentally validate candidate materials, does not claim discovery of new cathodes, and does not treat text-mined evidence as confirmation of electrochemical performance. The C-strict protocol is interpreted as a post-DFT decision-support protocol; target-specific leakage-corrected metrics are reported separately from full-feature reconstruction metrics. The shortlist should therefore be interpreted as a transparent follow-up set for future computational and experimental assessment.
